Utility - Trimmomatic

Trimmomatic

Trimmomatic performs a variety of useful trimming tasks for illumina paired-end and single ended data.The selection of trimming steps and their associated parameters are supplied on the command line.


Bolger, A. M., Lohse, M., & Usadel

Usadel Laboratory

Citation: Bolger, A. M., Lohse, M., & Usadel, B. (2014). Trimmomatic: A flexible trimmer for Illumina Sequence Data. Bioinformatics, 30 (15): 2114-2120. [link]
