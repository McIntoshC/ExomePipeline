BIC-seq

A CNA detection algorithm based on single-end whole-genome sequencing data for samples with matched controls. This algorithm, called BIC-seq, can accurately and efficiently identify the CNAs via minimizing the Bayesian information criterion (BIC)..


Peter J. Park, Center for Biomedical Informatics, Harvard Medical School
